Our Mission

GOLF TRIPS
Done Right.

We started CYNTP because we kept asking the same question every golfer asks: where should we go next? The answers were buried in forums, scattered across review sites, or just plain wrong.

So we went ourselves. We paid our own green fees, booked the same tee times you would, and started sharing what we actually found. That's the only way we could tell you the truth about any of it.

1
We Play Everything We Recommend
Every course in our directory and every trip we offer has been played and vetted by the CYNTP crew.
2
Honest Reviews, Always
We tell you what's great, what's overrated, and what to skip — regardless of who's paying.
3
Built for Real Golfers
Not tour pros, not bucket-listers with unlimited budgets. Golfers who want great trips at prices that make sense.
